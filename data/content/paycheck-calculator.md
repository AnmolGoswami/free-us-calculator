# California Paycheck Calculator 2026

Estimate your take-home pay in California after federal, state, and FICA taxes.

## What is a Paycheck Calculator?

A paycheck calculator helps you determine how much money you actually receive after taxes and deductions.

## Taxes Included

- Federal Income Tax
- California State Tax
- Social Security (6.2%)
- Medicare (1.45%)

## Why California?

California has one of the highest state tax rates in the US, making paycheck estimation very important.

## Example

If your salary is $80,000/year:

- Federal tax ≈ $17,600
- State tax ≈ $7,440
- FICA ≈ $6,120

Net salary ≈ $48,840
