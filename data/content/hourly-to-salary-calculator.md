# Hourly to Salary Calculator USA 2026

Convert your hourly wage into an annual salary instantly. This calculator includes overtime pay and estimated federal taxes.

## How It Works

We calculate your salary using:

- Standard 40-hour work week
- Overtime pay at 1.5x for hours above 40
- 52 working weeks per year
- Average US tax estimation

## Formula Used

Weekly Pay = (Regular Hours × Hourly Rate) + (Overtime Hours × 1.5 × Hourly Rate)

Yearly Salary = Weekly Pay × 52

## Example

If you earn $20/hour and work 45 hours/week:

- 40 hours at $20 = $800
- 5 hours at $30 = $150
- Weekly = $950
- Yearly = $49,400
