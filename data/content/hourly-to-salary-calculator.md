## How the Salary Calculator Works

This tool is designed for Indian work culture with flexible inputs:

- **Hours per week**: Choose 40 (standard), 45 (common in IT), or 48 hours
- **Days per week**: 5 or 6 days
- **Weeks per year**: 52 or adjusted for holidays and paid leaves
- **Overtime multiplier**: 1.5x or 2x as per company policy or law
- **Tax rate**: Flat effective percentage for quick net pay estimate

**Core Formulas Used:**
- Annual Salary = Hourly Rate × Hours per Week × Weeks per Year
- Hourly Rate = Annual Salary ÷ (Hours per Week × Weeks per Year)
- Daily Salary = Weekly Salary ÷ Days per Week
- Overtime Pay = Base Rate × Overtime Hours × Multiplier
- Net Pay = Gross Pay – Estimated Tax

## Salary Structure & Tax Information in India 2026

**Typical CTC Breakdown (₹12 Lakh Example):**
- Basic Salary: 40–50%
- House Rent Allowance (HRA): 40–50% of basic (tax benefits in some cities)
- Special Allowance: Remaining flexible component
- Employer PF Contribution: 12% of basic
- Gratuity and other benefits

**Common Deductions from Gross:**
- Employee PF: 12%
- Professional Tax: ₹200–₹300 per month
- Income Tax (TDS): Depends on slab and regime
- ESI (if applicable)

**New Tax Regime 2026** generally offers lower rates but limited deductions. Use this calculator with 10–30% effective tax rate for realistic estimates.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is ₹50,000 monthly salary in hourly rate in India?
At 48 hours per week: approximately ₹240 per hour.  
At 40 hours per week: approximately ₹288 per hour.

### How do I convert annual CTC to hourly rate?
Divide your Annual CTC by (Hours per week × 52).  
Example: ₹15 Lakh ÷ (45 × 52) ≈ ₹641 per hour.

### Does this calculator include overtime?
Yes. Switch to Overtime mode to add extra hours and choose 1.5x or 2x multiplier.

### Is overtime paid double in India?
Yes, for hourly and contract workers under the Factories Act and other labour laws. Salaried employees may get compensatory off or different rates as per company policy.

### How accurate is the tax calculation?
It uses a flat effective tax rate for quick estimates. For precise calculations, use a detailed Income Tax calculator considering deductions and regime.

### Can freelancers use this salary calculator?
Absolutely. Many freelancers and consultants use it to set competitive hourly rates or compare with full-time job offers.

### What is the best hours per week to assume?
40 hours is standard, but 45–48 hours is more realistic for most private sector jobs in India.
