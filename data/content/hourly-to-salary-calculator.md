## 🧠 How It Works

We calculate your salary using:

* Standard 40-hour work week
* 52 working weeks per year
* Optional overtime (1.5× pay rate)
* Estimated US tax percentage

---

## ⚙️ Formula Used

**Weekly Pay**
= (Regular Hours × Hourly Rate) + (Overtime Hours × 1.5 × Hourly Rate)

**Annual Salary**
= Weekly Pay × 52

**Monthly Salary**
= Annual Salary ÷ 12
