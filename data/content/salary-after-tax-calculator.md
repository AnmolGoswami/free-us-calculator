# Salary After Tax Calculator (USA 2026)

Calculate your take-home salary after federal tax, state tax, and FICA deductions.

## How It Works

This calculator estimates your net income based on:

- Federal income tax brackets
- State tax (varies by state)
- Social Security (6.2%)
- Medicare (1.45%)

## Example

If you earn $80,000 per year:

- Federal Tax: ~$13,000
- FICA: ~$6,120
- State Tax: ~$4,000

Net Salary: ~$56,880

## Why This Matters

Understanding your real income helps you:

- Plan your budget
- Compare job offers
- Estimate savings
- Manage expenses better

## Pro Tip

States like Texas and Florida have **0% state tax**, increasing your take-home pay significantly.
