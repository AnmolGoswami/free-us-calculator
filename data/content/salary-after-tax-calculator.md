## 🧮 How the 2026 Tax Calculation Works (In-Depth)

The tool follows the exact IRS process used in 2026:

1. **Gross Salary** → Start here
2. **Minus Pre-Tax Deductions** → Adjusted Gross Income (AGI)
3. **Minus Standard Deduction (2026 amounts)** → Taxable Income
4. **Apply 2026 Federal Progressive Brackets** → Federal Income Tax
5. **Minus Tax Credits** → Final Federal Tax
6. **Add FICA Taxes**:
   - Social Security: 6.2% (up to wage base of $184,500)
   - Medicare: 1.45%
   - Additional Medicare Tax: 0.9% on income above threshold
7. **Add State Income Tax** (flat rate for flat-tax states, estimated for progressive states)
8. **Final Result** = **Net Take-Home Pay**
