# DoorDash Earnings Calculator 2026

Estimate how much you can earn as a DoorDash driver using this free and accurate calculator designed for US gig workers.

## How DoorDash Earnings Work

Your income depends on:

- Number of deliveries per hour
- Earnings per order (base pay + tips)
- Total working hours per day
- Days worked per week

## Formula Used

Earnings = Orders per Hour × Pay per Order × Time Worked

## Example

If you complete 2 orders per hour and earn $8 per order:

- Hourly: $16
- Daily (6 hours): $96
- Weekly (5 days): $480
- Monthly: $1920

## Tips to Maximize Earnings

- Work during peak hours (lunch & dinner)
- Choose high-paying orders
- Avoid long-distance low-paying deliveries
- Track your fuel and maintenance costs
