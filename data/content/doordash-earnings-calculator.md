## Important Tax Information for DoorDash Drivers

As an independent contractor, DoorDash will send you a **1099-NEC** if you earn over $600 in a year. You are responsible for:

- Self-employment tax of **15.3%**
- Federal and state income taxes
- Quarterly estimated tax payments

**Pro Tip**: Keep detailed mileage logs. The IRS allows you to deduct **72.5 cents per mile** in 2026.
