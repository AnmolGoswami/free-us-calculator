## 2026 Self Employment Tax Rates & Rules Summary

- **SE Tax Rate**: 15.3% (12.4% SS + 2.9% Medicare)
- **Social Security Wage Base (2026)**: $184,500
- **Additional Medicare Tax**: 0.9% above $200k (Single/HoH) or $250k (Joint)
- **Standard Deductions 2026**:
  - Single: $16,100
  - Married Joint: $32,200
  - Head of Household: $24,150
- You can deduct **half** of your SE tax from your income
- Quarterly estimated taxes required if you expect to owe $1,000+
